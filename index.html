About

Welcome to the official website of Dr. Daniel J. Grace.

Dr. Grace is an author, journalist, researcher, and theological writer whose work focuses on Christian history, biblical studies, church history, early Christianity, faith, and contemporary society.

Through books, articles, digital media, and educational resources, he seeks to make historical and theological subjects accessible to a wider audience.
