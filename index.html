About

Dr. Daniel J. Grace is an author, journalist, researcher, and theological writer whose work explores Christian history, biblical studies, early Christianity, church history, faith, and contemporary society.

Through books, articles, research projects, and digital media, he seeks to make historical and theological knowledge accessible to a wider audience.
